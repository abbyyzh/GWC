Elizabeth Alebachew

Web Developer

Hi, I'm Liz! I am fifteen years old and I live in the greater Seattle area. I am an upcoming junior at Thomas Jefferson High School. I never really tried coding before Girls Who Code so I found this as an opportunity. I wanted to create something that I relate to deeply, like body image. I have dealt with this issue all of my life and I wanted to be able to help others by informing people about body image and different things that can affect other peoples perspective on body image. I wish there where more resources that inform people like this website when i grew up. I just hope this website can help others feel more confident in their own bodies!
